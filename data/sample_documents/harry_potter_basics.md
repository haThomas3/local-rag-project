# Harry Potter basic test knowledge

Harry Potter is an orphaned boy who lives with the Dursley family at 4 Privet Drive before discovering that he is a wizard.

On Harry's eleventh birthday, Rubeus Hagrid tells Harry that he is a wizard and delivers his Hogwarts letter.

Hogwarts is a school of witchcraft and wizardry. The four Hogwarts houses are Gryffindor, Slytherin, Ravenclaw, and Hufflepuff.

Harry becomes close friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger.

Albus Dumbledore is the headmaster of Hogwarts.

Lord Voldemort is the dark wizard who killed Harry's parents, James Potter and Lily Potter.

Severus Snape teaches Potions during Harry's first year at Hogwarts.

Quidditch is a magical sport played on broomsticks. Harry becomes a Seeker for Gryffindor.

The Philosopher's Stone is hidden at Hogwarts during Harry's first year.

The local RAG system should answer Harry Potter questions only when the answer appears in this file.
